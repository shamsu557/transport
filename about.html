About the Ministry

The Kano State Ministry of Transportation is responsible for developing, regulating, and maintaining an efficient transportation system that meets the needs of the state’s growing population and economy. With a commitment to innovation and sustainability, the ministry strives to create a well-structured and modern transport network that enhances mobility, improves road safety, and facilitates seamless movement of people and goods across the state.

At the heart of our efforts is a vision to establish a safe, integrated, and sustainable transportation framework that supports economic development and enhances the quality of life for all residents of Kano State. By leveraging modern technology, strategic planning, and stakeholder engagement, we aim to transform transportation infrastructure and services into a model of efficiency, safety, and environmental responsibility.

Our mission is to formulate and implement policies that ensure the smooth operation of road networks, public transportation systems, and traffic management solutions. We are dedicated to reducing congestion, enforcing safety regulations, and introducing smart mobility solutions that address the transportation challenges of an evolving urban landscape. Through proactive measures, we ensure that transport infrastructure remains accessible, reliable, and capable of meeting the demands of Kano State’s economic and social growth.

As a government body, we work closely with local, national, and international partners to develop strategic projects that enhance connectivity across urban and rural areas. Our initiatives include road expansion programs, traffic decongestion strategies, sustainable public transport solutions, and regulatory frameworks that encourage private sector participation in transportation development.

Beyond infrastructure development, we prioritize capacity building, training, and public awareness campaigns to promote road safety and responsible transport management. By fostering an efficient, inclusive, and resilient transport sector, we are committed to shaping a future where movement within Kano State is seamless, safe, and sustainable.
