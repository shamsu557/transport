About the Kano State Ministry of Transportation

Who We Are

The Kano State Ministry of Transportation is the government body responsible for planning, developing, and regulating the transportation sector in Kano. We are dedicated to creating a smart, safe, and efficient transport system that meets the needs of our growing population while driving economic and social development.

What We Do

We develop policies, implement infrastructure projects, and oversee transport operations to ensure Kano has a modern and accessible transportation system. Our work includes expanding road networks, improving railway connectivity, integrating smart mobility solutions, and promoting environmentally sustainable transport initiatives.

Railway Expansion: We are investing in railway development to enhance intercity and intracity transport, reducing road congestion and making travel more affordable and efficient.

Smart Mobility: We are introducing intelligent traffic management systems, cashless payment options for public transport, and enhanced ride-sharing services to improve urban transit.

Road Safety & Traffic Management: We are regulating and expanding road networks, enforcing safety measures, and using advanced technology to reduce congestion and improve traffic flow.

Sustainable Transport: Our policies focus on eco-friendly transportation solutions, reducing carbon emissions, and integrating green energy into Kano’s transport system.
