About the Ministry

The Kano Ministry of Transportation is responsible for the development, regulation, and maintenance of transportation infrastructure and services across the state. The ministry works to improve mobility, reduce traffic congestion, and ensure that public and private transport systems operate efficiently.

Through strategic policies and innovative solutions, the ministry oversees road infrastructure projects, public transportation initiatives, and traffic management systems to enhance the movement of people and goods within Kano State.
