Sectors in the Ministry of Transportation Kano State

Explore the various sectors dedicated to improving transportation in Kano State:

Road Transport and Mass Transit Administration

Oversees road transport services and the development of mass transit systems to enhance public mobility.

Traffic Management (KAROTA)

Enforces traffic regulations, manages congestion, and ensures road safety within Kano State.

Vehicle Inspection Office (VIO)

Handles vehicle inspection, roadworthiness certification, driver testing, and safety compliance.

Transport Policy and Planning

Develops and implements transport policies to improve mobility and connectivity across the state.

Engineering and Infrastructure Development

Oversees the construction and maintenance of transportation infrastructure, ensuring quality and safety.

Special Duties

Handles specific assignments and projects that require specialized attention within the transportation sector.

Reform Coordination and Service Improvement

Focuses on implementing reforms and enhancing service delivery within the Ministry of Transportation.
