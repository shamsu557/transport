Agencies Under the Kano Ministry of Transportation

These agencies play a crucial role in ensuring an efficient, safe, and well-regulated transportation system in Kano State.

Kano Road Traffic Agency (KAROTA)

Responsible for traffic management, enforcing road regulations, and ensuring safety on Kano roads.

Vehicle Inspection Office (VIO)

Handles vehicle inspections, roadworthiness certification, and driver testing to enhance road safety.

Kano Metropolitan Transport Authority (KAMATA)

Oversees urban transport policies, regulates commercial transport, and improves transit services.

Kano Line Transport Services

A government-owned transport service providing intercity and interstate travel options.

Motor Vehicle Administrative Agency(MVAA)

Handles vehicle registration, licensing, and administration of motor vehicle records.
