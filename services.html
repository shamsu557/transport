Our Services

Providing efficient, safe, and modern transportation solutions across Kano State.

Road Infrastructure Development

We construct, rehabilitate, and maintain roads and highways to enhance mobility.

Public Transport Management

Regulation and coordination of public transport services, including bus systems and tricycles.

Traffic Management & Road Safety

Implementation of traffic rules through KAROTA and promotion of road safety awareness campaigns.

Vehicle Inspection & Licensing

Inspection and certification of vehicles for roadworthiness through VIO services.

Emergency Transport Services

Quick response to road accidents in collaboration with health and security agencies.

Nigerian Institute of Transport Technology (NITT) Kano Annex

Empowering professionals with advanced transportation knowledge.

Located at Dawakin Kudu, Kano State, the NITT Kano Annex is a premier institution dedicated to research, training, and professional development in transportation and logistics.

Our specialized programs include:

  • Traffic Management & Road Safety
  • Urban Transport Planning
  • Public Transit Operations
  • Smart Mobility Solutions
